## THE REAL FILTER: SME DEPENDENCY

Every vertical falls into one of three categories based on how much of YOUR time and your MEDICAL TEAM's time is required:

### Category A: FULL AUTOPILOT (No medical SME needed)
Content is regulatory/legal — not clinical. AI generates from the regulation text itself. You review for accuracy against the published standard, not for medical judgment. A paralegal or compliance officer could review this. Or honestly, you can review it yourself in 30 minutes because the source material is the regulation, not medical literature.

### Category B: LIGHT TOUCH (Your RT/healthcare knowledge is sufficient)
Content overlaps with your existing healthcare expertise. You or one of your existing writers can spot-check without engaging a new specialist. Leverages content you already have or know.

### Category C: MEDICAL SME REQUIRED (Need specialist review)
Content requires clinical expertise you don't personally have. Must involve a credentialed professional for accuracy and liability reasons. These are the ones to defer.

## SOLO BUILDER SCORING MODEL

For each Category A and B vertical, I'm scoring on 5 factors (1-10 each, max 50):

1. **Market Size** — Total US market revenue potential
2. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** — How easy/cheap to reach buyers
3. **Build Time** — Calendar days from decision to live
4. **Automation Level** — How much runs without you after launch
5. **Revenue Per Hour of Your Time** — The real metric for a solo builder

### Scoring Definitions

**Market Size (1-10):**
10 = $1B+ | 8 = $500M-1B | 6 = $100-500M | 4 = $50-100M | 2 = <$50M

**CAC (1-10, inverted — low CAC = high score):**
10 = Buyers search for this (mandatory, they MUST buy) | 8 = High search volume, clear intent
6 = Moderate search, some education needed | 4 = Requires outbound/education | 2 = Hard to find buyers

**Build Time (1-10, inverted — fast = high score):**
10 = <1 week | 8 = 1-2 weeks | 6 = 2-4 weeks | 4 = 1-2 months | 2 = 3+ months

**Automation Level (1-10):**
10 = Set and forget after launch | 8 = Monthly check-in | 6 = Weekly light touch
4 = Weekly significant involvement | 2 = Daily involvement needed

**Revenue Per Hour of Your Time (1-10):**
Based on estimated revenue ÷ estimated hours of YOUR time per year to maintain

## WHY THIS ORDER — THE REASONING

### #1: HIPAA Training (Score: 44)
- **18M healthcare workers MUST have this every year.** Not optional. Not nice-to-have. Federal law.
- **Buyers actively search for it.** "HIPAA training online" has massive search volume. Zero education needed — they already know they need it.
- **Content source is the regulation itself.** 45 CFR Parts 160 and 164. AI reads the law, explains it in plain language, creates scenarios. No medical judgment needed.
- **Annual recurrence.** Every learner comes back every year. Built-in retention.
- **Bundle anchor.** Every hospital that buys RT CE from you ALSO needs HIPAA for those same employees. This is the gateway to the employer bundle.
- **Your time:** ~8 hours to launch. ~2 hours/month ongoing. AI monitors for regulatory updates (rare — HIPAA doesn't change often). Revenue potential with 0.01% market capture: $90K/yr.

### #2: Bloodborne Pathogens (Score: 44)
- **Identical logic to HIPAA** but the content is even simpler. OSHA 29 CFR 1910.1030 is extremely prescriptive — it literally tells you what the training must cover.
- **Virtually zero content risk.** The standard lists the exact topics. AI generates from a checklist.
- **Perfect bundle with HIPAA.** Most employers need both. "HIPAA + BBP bundle: $29.99" is an obvious product.
- **Your time:** ~6 hours to launch. ~1 hour/month ongoing. The regulation almost never changes.

### #3: Sexual Harassment Prevention (Score: 42)
- **$2B market.** 6+ states MANDATE it for all employers. California alone has 19M workers.
- **State approval needed but EASY.** Not "accreditation" — just filing with the state labor department. AI generates the application. You sign and submit. Approvals typically take 1-4 weeks.
- **Content is statutory.** Each state defines exactly what must be covered (protected classes, reporting procedures, retaliation, bystander intervention). AI reads the statute, generates training.
- **MASSIVE search volume.** "Sexual harassment training California" / "New York" / "Illinois" — these are high-intent, high-volume queries.
- **Sells to EVERY employer**, not just healthcare. This is your first non-healthcare vertical.
- **Your time:** ~12 hours to launch (includes state filing for CA + NY + IL). ~2 hours/month.

### #4: Cybersecurity Awareness (Score: 42)
- **$2.5B market.** The single largest compliance training category.
- **No accreditation AT ALL.** No state filing. No regulatory approval. Just build and sell.
- **Insurance companies increasingly require it.** Cyber insurance policies mandate annual training. That's a backdoor mandate that affects millions of businesses.
- **Content is standardized.** NIST Cybersecurity Framework, phishing recognition, password hygiene, social engineering. Well-documented, non-clinical.
- **Sells to EVERY employer.** Expands your addressable market beyond healthcare entirely.
- **Competitive but undifferentiated.** Most cybersecurity training is boring. AI + good UX = differentiation.
- **Your time:** ~8 hours to launch. ~2 hours/month (update for new threat types quarterly).

### #5: Infection Control (Score: 42)
- **The first one that touches your healthcare expertise** — but lightly. CMS Conditions of Participation + CDC guidelines. Your background in healthcare education is more than sufficient.
- **Same buyers as HIPAA and BBP.** The hospital compliance officer buying HIPAA + BBP also needs infection control. Triple bundle.
- **Your time:** ~10 hours (slightly more because you'll want to review the clinical accuracy yourself). ~2 hours/month.

### #6: AI Literacy — Cross-Profession (Score: 40)
- **This is your unfair advantage.** You're literally building AI systems. You understand this better than 99% of course providers.
- **No competitor owns this space.** Nobody has "AI Literacy CE" as a brand across professions.
- **PREMIUM pricing.** AI training commands $20-50/person vs. $10-15 for commodity compliance. It's new, it's in-demand, and nobody has a definitive course yet.
- **Sells to every profession.** The same core content (what is AI, how it works, ethical implications, professional impact) adapts to any audience with light customization.
- **Your time:** ~15 hours (you'll want to be more hands-on here because this IS your brand). But the payoff is disproportionate.

## ONE-PAGE DECISION FRAMEWORK

When evaluating ANY future vertical, score it on these 5 questions:

1. **Can AI generate the content from a published regulation/standard?** (Yes = Category A = go fast)
2. **Do buyers actively search for this training?** (Yes = low CAC = prioritize)
3. **Is it recurring (annual/biennial)?** (Yes = retention = prioritize)
4. **Can it bundle with existing verticals?** (Yes = higher ARPU = prioritize)
5. **Does it need medical SME review?** (No = solo buildable = prioritize)

If a vertical scores Yes on all 5, launch it this month.
If it scores Yes on 4/5, launch it this quarter.
If it scores Yes on 3/5, put it in the backlog.
If it scores below 3, don't bother unless strategic.

Every vertical in your Sprint 1-3 scores 5/5 or 4/5. That's why they're first.
